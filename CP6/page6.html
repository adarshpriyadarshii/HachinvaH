Amadubi Village, Jharkhand

About

Amadubi Rural Tourism Village is home to talented tribal artists who are known for the traditional Paitkar Scroll Paintings. The village is home to around 54 chitrakar families who are skilled in painting scenes from epics, folklore and village life on scrolls made from leaves and barks of trees. A visit to this artists' hub makes for an enriching experience for tourists as they can directly visit the homes of the artists and choose from a wide selection of attractive Paitkar Scroll Paintings. The artists have been following this age-old traditional art and their skill and finesse are unparalleled. You can buy some amazing paintings to take back home as souvenirs. The village lies in Dhalbhumgarh block of East Singhbhum and is a hub of ethnic artists of Jharkhand.
